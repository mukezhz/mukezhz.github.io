## Introduction

Hi, I’m Mukesh Kumar Chaudhary — CTO at Astergaze Technology.

I work with an amazing team to build digital products in **insurance** and **abroad study**. My role sits across product, engineering, and execution: turning ideas into useful, reliable software that creates real value for users and the business.

## What I Do

I’m product-focused first, technology-focused second.

That means I care most about:

- solving the right problems,
- building what users actually need,
- and delivering consistently with quality.

At Astergaze, I focus on:

- shaping product and technology direction,
- building strong engineering culture and ownership,
- improving delivery speed and system reliability,
- and keeping product, engineering, and business aligned.

## How I Think

I believe good products are built through clarity, fast feedback, and continuous improvement.

My usual approach:

- understand the user and business problem deeply,
- keep solutions simple and practical,
- ship in iterations,
- measure outcomes,
- and keep improving.

## A Bit About Me

I started my software journey through curiosity and stayed in it because I love solving meaningful problems. Over the years, I’ve worked across backend systems, frontend applications, infrastructure, and team leadership — but today, my biggest strength is connecting **product thinking** with **engineering execution**.
